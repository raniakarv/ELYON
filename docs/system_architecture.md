# ELYON System Architecture

## Goal

The goal of ELYON is to prioritize natural bioactive compounds for skin health using biological evidence from publicly available scientific databases.

---

# Workflow

Compound

↓

Biological Databases

↓

Evidence Collection

↓

Evidence Scoring Framework

↓

Compound Ranking

↓

Explainable Report

---

# Main Components

## 1. Compound Database

Stores information about natural compounds.

Examples:
- Curcumin
- Resveratrol
- EGCG

---

## 2. Biological Evidence

Collects evidence from biological databases.

Examples:
- Target proteins
- Biological pathways
- Gene interactions
- Scientific publications

---

## 3. Evidence Scoring Framework

Transforms biological evidence into quantitative scores.

Output:

Overall Evidence Score

---

## 4. Explainability Module

Explains why each compound received its score.

---

## 5. Visualization

Displays rankings, scores and biological evidence.
