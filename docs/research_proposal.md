# Research Proposal

## Project Title

AI-Assisted Discovery of Natural Bioactive Compounds for Skin Health

---

## Background

The cosmetics and pharmaceutical industries are increasingly shifting toward evidence-based discovery of bioactive compounds. Public biological databases and scientific literature contain valuable information that can be integrated using computational biology and artificial intelligence to identify promising natural compounds for skin health.

---

## Research Question

Can publicly available biological and chemical data be integrated using computational methods to prioritize natural bioactive compounds for skin health?
