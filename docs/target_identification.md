# Target Identification Workflow

## Objective

Identify the primary molecular targets of a bioactive compound before studying their biological functions.

---

## Data Source

Comparative Toxicogenomics Database (CTD)

Purpose:
- Identify genes associated with a compound.
- Explore chemical–gene interactions.
- Evaluate literature support.

---

## Selection Strategy

Not every reported interaction should be included.

Target genes are selected based on:

- Number of reported interactions
- Literature support
- Biological relevance
- Relevance to skin biology
- Involvement in oxidative stress
- Involvement in inflammation

---

## Example: Curcumin

CTD reports:

- 1,077 associated genes

The first candidate targets selected for further investigation are:

- NFE2L2
- TNF
- PTGS2
- MMP9
- HMOX1

These targets will be validated and annotated using UniProt in the next step.
